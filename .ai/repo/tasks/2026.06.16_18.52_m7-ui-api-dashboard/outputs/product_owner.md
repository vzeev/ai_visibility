# Product Owner Output

## Verdict

Approved for implementation.

## Product Framing

- target user: interviewer or technical stakeholder evaluating an AI visibility
  platform demo.
- desired outcome: quickly understand configuration, queue health, raw evidence,
  and derived insight state.
- critical journey: select configured brand/prompt set, create or inspect a run,
  search raw responses, inspect derived summaries and evidence.

## UX Direction

- Prioritize dense operational clarity over landing-page composition.
- Use Brandlight-like signals: clean white/light surfaces, blue accents, compact
  filters, metrics, tables, and evidence drilldown.
- Keep failures readable because local services may not be running.
- Do not use copied Brandlight assets.

## Non-Goals

- Full CRUD UI.
- Auth or account settings.
- Marketing hero page.
